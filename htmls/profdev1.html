Technopreneurship

Turning Technology into Business Opportunities

Roles and Assignments

I was originally designated to handle the technical side of the “Technopreneurship” webinar, ensuring the virtual event ran smoothly and troubleshooting any real-time issues. However, due to my simultaneous appointment for facilitating DRRM training for first-year students, I was unable to be present onsite.

Collaboration on Narrative Report

Recognizing my limited availability for technical duty, I volunteered to support the documentation team by helping compose the official narrative report for the webinar. Working closely with Ma. Louicel Quong, I clarified key points, added context, and helped distill the core takeaways from our activity.

Event Day Adjustments

On the day of the event, I was set to deliver the closing remarks. However, our group made a last-minute change, and I was reassigned to read out the certificate citations instead. This unexpected shift required me to quickly adapt and fulfill the task professionally, despite the sudden modification of plans.

“Flexibility and collaboration were essential—not only in planning but also in executing responsibilities as circumstances changed.”

Reflection and Takeaways

  • Demonstrated adaptability, moving from a technical role to a documentation and support role.
  • Enhanced the narrative report for clarity and depth through collaboration.
  • Navigated last-minute program changes with professionalism and composure.
  • Strengthened teamwork skills through communication and flexibility.
