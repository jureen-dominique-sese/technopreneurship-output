Use of Thermal Camera for Electromechanical Inspection

Introduction

This seminar was made possible by the Bicol University Electrical Engineering Department, in partnership with the IIEE - UAE Chapter. The event was part of the ProfDev Series focused on emerging technologies. The first session was a virtual lecture by Engr. Ferdinand B. Borlongan on using thermal cameras for inspection.

Summary

Engr. Borlongan provided an accessible overview of how thermal cameras are used for electromechanical troubleshooting. He highlighted their ability to detect anomalies like overheating components, faulty connections, and insulation defects, thereby preventing costly breakdowns.

Learnings

I learned that thermal cameras are essential for preventative maintenance in electromechanical systems. The practical demonstrations and case studies clearly illustrated their effectiveness in real-world applications, ensuring operational efficiency and safety.

Basic Solar Design and Installation

Introduction

I attended this seminar on Basic Solar Design, featuring speaker Engr. Constantine Reymundo. Despite joining a bit late due to an internet outage, I found the content highly engaging and participated in an interactive quiz at the end.

Summary

The webinar covered the intricacies of solar design, including types of solar panels, efficiency ratings, and the fundamental components of photovoltaic systems like inverters and batteries. It also touched on system sizing, site assessment, and regulatory requirements in the Philippines.

Learnings

I learned that solar design is a multi-faceted business requiring significant investment. The session was informative about current trends and the future of solar energy in the Philippines, emphasizing its economic and environmental benefits.

Technopreneurship: Turning Technology into Business

Introduction & Role

Our group hosted this webinar. I was initially assigned to the technical team but had a schedule conflict with DRRM training. I pivoted to support the documentation team, co-authoring the narrative report with Ma. Louicel Quong to ensure the event's key takeaways were captured.

Summary of Experience

On the event day, my role shifted again from delivering closing remarks to reading certificate citations. This last-minute change required quick adaptation and professionalism.

Learnings

The seminar taught me how to identify market gaps and leverage innovation for business. As an organizer, I also learned the importance of clear communication and flexible role assignments to ensure a successful event despite unforeseen challenges.
