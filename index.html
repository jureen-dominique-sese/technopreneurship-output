Use of Thermal Camera for Electromechanical Inspection

Introduction

This seminar, a collaboration between the Bicol University Electrical Engineering Department and the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers - UAE Chapter, was a deep dive into the practical applications of thermal imaging. As part of the 'Charting the Frontiers of Emerging Technologies in Electrical Engineering' series, it promised to be an insightful session, and it delivered.

Summary

Engr. Borlongan's presentation was a masterclass in clarity. He didn't just explain the 'what' of thermal imaging but the 'why' and 'how.' We learned to interpret thermal signatures, identify potential failure points in electrical systems, and understand the nuances of predictive maintenance. The case studies were particularly illuminating, showing how this technology saves companies millions in downtime and repairs.

Learnings

My key takeaway was the proactive power of thermal imaging. It's not just about fixing problems; it's about preventing them. This technology allows for a shift from reactive to predictive maintenance, a concept that is revolutionizing the industry. I am now inspired to explore this technology further and see how I can incorporate it into my future projects.

Basic Solar Design and Installation

Introduction

Despite a rocky start with an internet outage, I was determined to catch as much of Engr. Constantine Reymundo's seminar on solar design as possible. The energy in the virtual room was palpable, and I was eager to learn from an expert in the field.

Summary

The webinar was a comprehensive overview of the solar landscape. We covered everything from the physics of photovoltaic cells to the economics of solar installations. The discussion on government incentives and the future of renewable energy in the Philippines was particularly insightful, highlighting the immense potential for growth in this sector.

Learnings

I now have a much clearer understanding of the complexities of solar design. It's a field that requires a blend of technical expertise, financial acumen, and a deep understanding of environmental factors. The seminar has given me the confidence to explore this field further and perhaps even consider a career in renewable energy.

This experience was a crash course in event management. I learned the importance of clear communication, contingency planning, and the ability to think on your feet.
